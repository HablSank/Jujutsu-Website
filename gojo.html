GOJO SATORU

Gojo Satoru is a central character in Jujutsu Kaisen, renowned for his overwhelming strength and confident personality. As one of the most powerful jujutsu sorcerers, Gojo possesses the unique Limitless technique and the Six Eyes, which give him unparalleled control over cursed energy. Known for his playful yet often arrogant demeanor, he is deeply protective of his students and strives to reshape the jujutsu world. Gojo's strength and vision make him a key figure in the fight against curses and a symbol of hope for the future of jujutsu sorcery.
