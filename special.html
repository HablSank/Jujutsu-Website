Special Grade Sorcerers

It is reserved for sorcerers with such immense strength that their destructive potential is immeasurable, making them very exclusive and unique cases, hence the title "special". As a testament to its rarity, as of 2018, there are only 4 registered sorcerers with the Special Grade rank.
