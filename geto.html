GETO SUGURU

Geto Suguru is a complex antagonist in Jujutsu Kaisen, known for his charisma, cunning, and dark ideology. Once a promising jujutsu sorcerer and close friend of Satoru Gojo, he became disillusioned with humanity, leading him to embrace a radical vision of eliminating non-sorcerers. A master of Cursed Spirit Manipulation, Geto wields immense power and commands a following of cursed spirits and fanatics. His tragic fall from grace and ideological conviction make him a compelling and formidable figure in the series.
